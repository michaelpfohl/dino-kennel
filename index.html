Feed

Feed your Dino for +10 health! (I hear they really like tourist legs, but maybe you can teach them to give up meat, for the environment!)

Pet

Pet your Dino for +3 health! (Just watch your hands though, those teeth are real sharp and if you haven't fed them in a while things could get dicey!)

Adventure

Send your Dino on an adventure! Costs 20 health but experiences are priceless! Just be careful not to let your Dino get to low on health!

Remove

Remove your dino from the page! (I know it can be hard to say goodbye but they are giant reptiles so maybe letting them go is for the best.)

View

Learn even more about your Dino! See what adventures they have been on and when and get a closer look at what kind of Dino they are!
